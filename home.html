Unspoken Words

Medium: Coding - p5.js, Art - AI Image - Adobe Firefly, Dimensions: 600 x 800


Demo of Unspoken Words

The concept of Unspoken Words is a visual novel like game in witch you would interact with the character
who lacks words to response back. Instead you would see their response based on the background shifting.

Game Rules

1. Click through the scenes

2. Pick given options to respond to Ghost

3. Try out the different options

4. Remember to have a good time

Concept

Unspoken Words's concept started off from what if you tried to speak your mind out,
but the words you fail to convey those exact words with emotion and it falls into a mess
of a smeared paint combined around. This project serves to define *Unspoken Words* through
video game medium where you as the audience interacts with the mysterious hooded finger to
past time around. Depending on the options you pick the hooded finger will react to your
choice with the background shifting between AI imagery as a symbolism to its lack
of words in attempts to express how they feel about your response.

AI Image Creation

I used Adobe Firefly to be able to have a wide range of different imagery to express each emotion:
angry, disgust, fear, happy, sad and suprised. I had to control and iterate around the many options
for how I wanted each emotion to be expressed with a bit of vagueness. Hence why the chosen images
seem like it could have trailed off from each other for emotions aren't just one layer but multiple
layers where the human mind can have a mix of emotions between the main emotions expressed.
